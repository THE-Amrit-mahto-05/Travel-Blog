INDIA

India, with over 1.4 billion people, is a land of rich history, culture, and natural beauty. Explore the iconic Taj Mahal, Rajasthan’s palaces, Kerala’s backwaters, and Goa’s beaches. Discover Delhi’s historic landmarks or the spiritual ghats of Varanasi. The Himalayas offer adventure, while vibrant markets and flavorful cuisine captivate visitors. With Hindi and English widely spoken, and October to March as the best travel period, India promises a diverse and unforgettable journey.
